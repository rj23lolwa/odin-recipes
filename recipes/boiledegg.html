Boiled Egg recipe

Description

Boiledd egg is a super healthy non-veg dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 Chicken Egg
  • 500 ml Water

Steps

  1. Take 500ml water in a pan
  2. Put it on a high flame
  3. Add the egg into it
  4. Let it boil for 8 minutes
  5. After 8 minutes take the egg out
  6. Remove its shell
  7. Now its fully prepared, serve hot!
