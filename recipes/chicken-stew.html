Chicken-Stew recipe

Description

Chicken-stew is a healthy and quick recipe.

Ingredients

  • 6 Chicken Pieces
  • 500 ml Water
  • 2 Chilles, 1 Garlic, 1 Onion, 1 Ginger, 1 Carrot
  • Cumin Powder, Coriander Powder, Turmeric Powder
  • Cornflour

Steps

  1. Cut the onion, chilli, ginger, carrot and garlic in small pieces
  2. Take some oil on a pan and put it on a oven
  3. Add the veggies you cut in the pan
  4. Add chicken pieces to it
  5. Let it fry for sometimes untill it turns brown
  6. Add the powders to it
  7. Add the water and close the pan with a lid and keep it on medium flame for 20 min
  8. Add cornflour and stir it
  9. Now its fully prepared, serve hot!
