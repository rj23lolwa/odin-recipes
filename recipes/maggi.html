Maggi recipe

Description

Maggi is delicious fast food.

Ingredients

  • 2 packets of Maggi
  • 500 ml Water

Steps

  1. Take a pan and add 500ml water in it
  2. Put the pan on a oven with high flame
  3. Once it starts boiling add the Maggi Noddles in the pan
  4. Let it boil for 3 to 4 minutes
  5. Add the masala to it and mix it propely
  6. Cook untill the water contents become very less
  7. Take it out in a plate and serve hot!
